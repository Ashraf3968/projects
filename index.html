About Velora

Designing environments that feel effortlessly refined.

Velora Interiors is a boutique studio trusted by collectors, hospitality leaders, and visionaries who expect a seamless, white-glove experience from concept to final styling.

Our Story

Founded in 2012, Velora became known for translating heritage architecture into modern sanctuaries. Every project is led by our principal designers with a meticulous eye for craftsmanship.

Mission

To create interiors that reflect the identity of our clients while advancing sustainable luxury through conscious sourcing, artisanal partnerships, and intelligent planning.

Why Clients Choose Us

  • Exclusive access to global ateliers and bespoke makers.
  • Precision project management with weekly white-glove updates.
  • Immersive 3D visualization before a single piece is fabricated.
